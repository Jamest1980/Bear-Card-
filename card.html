Grizzly Bear

Brown Bears are generally loners who will only seek a mate for short periods. At birth, bear cubs are blind and naked.

  • Scientific Name: Urses arctos horribilis
  • Average Height: 8 feet standing
  • Average Lifespan: 20-30 years
  • Habitat: Mountains and prairies

Yellowstone is home to two species of bears.The Grizzly bears have a much smaller range across the United States. The Grizzly Bear is larger that the black bear ans has a larger muscle mass above its shoulders.
